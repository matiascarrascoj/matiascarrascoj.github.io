Hi there! My name is Matías, I’m currently finishing my B.S.c in Economics at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). As of April 2025, I find myself doing my final semester at UNAM. Previously (Winter 2024-2025) I was an exchange student at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.

From October 2022 to October 2024 I had the privilege of receiving a scholarship from the National Council of Humanities, Science and Technology (CONAHCYT), working as a research assistant for Dr. Fernando Alberto Cortés Cáceres, at UNAM’s Development Studies Program (PUED). Since November 2024 I’ve been working, also at PUED, as a research assistant for Dr. Iliana Yaschine Arroyo.

My research interests are centered around development economics, demand-led growth theories and the political economy of neoclassical theory.

I’m interested in pursuing an academic career in economics after graduate school. I'm about to start a graduate EPOG-JMMSc in Economics (September 2025 - June 2027). I will follow the B2 minor (Socioeconomic and ecological economics) at Roma Tre University, as an Erasmus Mundus Scholar.
