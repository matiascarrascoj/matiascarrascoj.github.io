Hi there! My name is Matías, I’m currently finishing my B.S.c in Economics at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM). As of January 2025, I find myself doing an exchange semester at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.

From October 2022 to October 2024 I had the privilege of receiving a scholarship from the National Council of Humanities, Science and Technology (CONAHCYT), working as a research assistant for Dr. Fernando Alberto Cortés Cáceres, at UNAM’s Development Studies Program (PUED). Since November 2024 I’ve been working, also at PUED, as a research assistant for Dr. Iliana Yaschine Arroyo.

My research interests are centered around multidimensional poverty, income inequality, development economics and the political economy of neoclassical theory.

I’m interested in pursuing an academic career in economics after graduate school. I’m currently looking to apply to M.S.c programs in economics in the European Union for the incoming 2025 winter semester.
